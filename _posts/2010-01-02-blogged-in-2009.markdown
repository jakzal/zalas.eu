Up to now I spread my focus to the three blogs:



	
  * [private blog in English](http://www.zalas.eu)

	
  * [](http://www.zalas.eu)[private blog in Polish](http://www.zalas.pl)

	
  * [](http://www.zalas.pl)[GOYELLO blog](http://blog.goyello.com)
